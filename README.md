CoverPlay is the intuitive media companion designed to bring order to your video collection. Whether you are browsing locally stored content or revisiting your viewing habits, CoverPlay provides a sleek, dark-themed interface that keeps your focus on your media.
​Key Features:
​Centralized Organization: Easily categorize and access videos from various sources, such as your camera roll or downloads, all in one streamlined dashboard.
​Intuitive History Tracking: Never lose track of what you have watched. Our detailed history view keeps a log of your viewing sessions, including duration and file details, helping you pick up right where you left off.
​Simplified Navigation: Designed with a clean, mobile-first layout, ensuring that managing your library—from searching to filtering—is effortless and responsive.
​Elegant Dark Mode: Enjoy a modern, eye-friendly design that makes browsing your video library a comfortable experience at any time of day.
